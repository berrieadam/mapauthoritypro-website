Reviews drive real bookings
Studies show 88% of consumers trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations. A business going from 15 to 60 reviews typically sees 25–40% more inbound calls from Google.

Lost estimates are pure profit
You already did the work of generating the lead and writing the quote. Recovering one silent quote per month at $350 average job value is $4,200 per year — from follow-up texts that cost you nothing.

Reactivation is the highest-ROI channel
Existing customers are 5x more likely to book again than a cold lead. A quarterly reactivation campaign to 50 past customers typically generates 5–8 bookings — from people who already trust you.
