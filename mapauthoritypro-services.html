⭐Service 1

Review request automation

The core of your reputation system. Every completed job automatically triggers a satisfaction check — and routes the response intelligently. Happy customers go to Google. Unhappy ones go to a private form.

⏱️
Fires 90 minutes after the job ends — enough time for any overrun but while the experience is still fresh in the customer's mind. This timing is what makes the response rate 3x higher than asking days later.
🔀
Satisfaction gate routes every response correctly — YES gets the Google review link. NO gets a private feedback form and an urgent owner alert. No reply gets one gentle follow-up and nothing more.
🔄
3-touch follow-up for review requests — SMS on day 1, day 3, and day 6 if no review is detected. Sequence stops automatically the moment a review lands. Includes opt-out on every message.
🛡️
Fully compliant — no star gating — we never ask "rate us 1–5 stars and only click here if you rated 5." That practice violates Google's policies. Our satisfaction check is a yes/no question — completely above board.

📸

📝
8 Google Posts per month — fresh posts signal an active business to Google's algorithm. We write and schedule them every month. Includes seasonal promotions, service highlights, and tips.

🤖Service 3

AI review auto-replies

Every Google review gets a personalized reply within minutes of posting — whether it's 5 stars or 1 star. Google rewards businesses that respond to reviews. Most service businesses respond to fewer than 30% — usually only the negative ones.

⚡
Posts within minutes — not days later. Google's algorithm notes response time. Responding within hours signals an active, engaged business and positively affects local ranking.
🎭
Tone matches the star rating — 4–5 star reviews get a warm, genuine thank-you that reflects the specific service mentioned. 1–3 star reviews get an empathetic, professional recovery response inviting offline resolution.
🔗
Connected directly to your Google Business Profile — we connect GHL to your GBP and replies post through your account. They appear as coming from the business owner, not a third party.
📲
Owner alert on every review — you get an SMS the moment a review comes in so you always know what's being said about your business, even though you don't need to do anything about it.

Lost estimate recovery

You send a quote. The customer goes quiet. You move on and assume they went elsewhere. Most of the time that's wrong — they just got busy and forgot. Three automatic follow-ups recover an average of 28% of silent quotes. No other reputation platform offers this.

🎯
Triggers when contractor marks quote as sent — one field update in the app. The sequence fires automatically from that moment. No CSV uploads, no manually setting reminders.
📅
3 touches over 7 days — Day 2 is a soft check-in. Day 5 asks if they have any questions. Day 7 is a final gentle close. The sequence stops the instant the customer replies or accepts.
💬
SMS feels personal, not automated — each message is written to sound like the owner following up personally. First name, business name, reference to the quote. Customers reply to these because they don't feel like spam.
🔒
Unique to MapAuthorityPro — Podium, Birdeye, and NiceJob do not offer lost estimate follow-up. This is a feature we built because contractors told us it was their biggest revenue leak.

🎁Service 6

Referral campaign automation

7 days after a confirmed 5-star review, a referral ask goes out automatically. This timing is deliberate — it's when the customer's satisfaction is at its peak and they're most likely to recommend you. An 8% referral rate means 1 referral for every 12 happy customers contacted.

⏰
Timing is the secret — asking for a referral immediately after a job feels transactional. Asking 7 days after they've voluntarily left a 5-star review — when they're clearly still happy — feels natural. That's why it converts.
🎯
Only targets confirmed promoters — the referral ask only fires after a 5-star review is confirmed on Google. Customers who replied NO to the satisfaction check never receive a referral request.
💬
Owner notified immediately on referral reply — when a customer replies with a friend's name and number, you get an instant SMS alert so you can follow up while the referral is still warm.
🔁
Completely automated, no manual tracking — no spreadsheet of who to ask, no reminders to follow up. The campaign runs itself every time a new 5-star review comes in.
