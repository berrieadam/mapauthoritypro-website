The review flow

What happens after
every single job

This is the core sequence. It fires automatically every time an appointment ends in the booking system.

📱
Contractor books appointment
Opens Lead Connector app → searches customer → books time slot. Customer gets a confirmation SMS immediately.
🔧
Job is completed
Contractor finishes the work and leaves. Nothing manual needed — the system tracks the appointment end time automatically.
⏱️
Wait 90 minutes
1.5 hour buffer after scheduled end time covers any job overruns. Review request never fires while the contractor is still on site.
💬
Satisfaction check SMS fires
"Hi [Name]! It's [Business] — hope everything went great today! Were you happy with our service? Reply YES or NO 😊"
YES reply
Review request sent
"That's amazing! Would you mind leaving us a Google review? Takes 30 seconds: [link]" — then 2 follow-ups over 5 days if no review yet.
NO reply
Private feedback form sent
"We're sorry to hear that — can you tell us what went wrong?" — goes to a private form. Owner gets an urgent alert. Never touches Google.
⭐
Review detected — auto-reply posts
The moment a Google review comes in, an AI-generated personalized reply posts within minutes. Owner gets an SMS alert. Customer tagged for the referral campaign 7 days later.
