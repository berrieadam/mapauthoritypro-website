The problem

Your competitors are winning
on Google. Here's why.

Most service businesses lose customers every day without knowing it. Google drives 80% of local service searches — and the business with the most reviews wins.

01
Your profile is invisible
An unverified or incomplete Google Business Profile gets pushed down in local search. Customers looking for a plumber or HVAC tech nearby never see you — they see your competitor first.
02
Reviews go to the loudest, not the best
Happy customers don't leave reviews unless you ask. Unhappy ones do it automatically. Without a system asking every customer after every job, your rating stays stuck while competitors pull ahead.
03
Quotes disappear into silence
You send a quote. The customer goes quiet. You move on. Most contractors lose 30–40% of their quotes to simple follow-up failure — not because the customer wasn't interested, but because life got in the way.
04
Old customers forget you exist
A customer you serviced 18 months ago books a competitor next time — not because they were unhappy, but because nobody reached out. Reactivation campaigns recover this revenue automatically.
